Daily UI Challenge

The Daily UI Challenge is a set of design prompts aimed to help participants improve their UI design skills. The challenge lasts for 100 days and a new prompt is given each day. Although the challenge overlooks the importance of testing and research and the prompts were rather vague, it was fun to experiment with different styles and become more familiar with Figma.

Here are some of my designs that I created before I had to pause the challenge to prioritise my university studies.
